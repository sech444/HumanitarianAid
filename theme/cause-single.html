Cause Details

Build for school for poor child

  • Goal: $890
  • Raised: $390

At The International Mothers for Humanitarian Aid, we are embarking on a transformative mission to build a school for underprivileged children, providing them with a safe and nurturing environment where they can learn, grow, and thrive. Our goal is to create a space that fosters education, empowerment, and opportunities for a brighter future.

We Need Your Help.Let's Work together.

Your support is vital in turning this vision into reality. By contributing to our school-building initiative, you can make a direct and lasting impact on the lives of these children. Every donation, no matter how small, brings us one step closer to providing a life-changing opportunity for these young minds.

Our vision for the school goes beyond providing basic education. We envision a holistic learning environment that not only imparts knowledge but also fosters critical thinking, creativity, and essential life skills. Through a comprehensive curriculum and dedicated educators, we aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are equipped to overcome challenges and contribute positively to their communities.
